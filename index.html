Gameplay
How it plays
▶
A network map of connected nodes — politicians, police, developers, judges, press
▶
Each turn you gather evidence, reveal connections, and map who answers to whom
▶
Evidence cards surface real document types: wire transfers, witness statements, procurement files
▶
The network is too large to see whole at once — that's the point
Network map — Turn 3 of 8
Mechanics
The decisions
that matter
⚠
Accuse when you have enough evidence — but you might be wrong
$
Accept a bribe, document it, or refuse — each shifts your integrity and exposure
○
Archive a case and walk away — sometimes that's the deal you made
▶
Rank, funds, and integrity at the end reflect every choice along the way
Bribe modal with evidence card
Ending
How it ends
—
No win condition — only outcomes
—
The ending screen reveals the full network and your position in it
—
Each outcome maps to a real-world parallel — Romania, Bulgaria, North Macedonia
Dismantled
Corruption exposed. National investigation triggered. Chief shortlist.
Compliant
Promoted inside a compromised system. The network survived.
Absorbed
Took the money. Became part of the web. Chief anyway.
Outlasted
Evidence disappeared. Time ran out. Still a Detective.

◈

◈
